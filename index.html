Sustainability
DMG International is steadfast in its commitment to sustainability and actively supports the global transition towards a low-carbon future, in alignment with the commitments outlined in the Paris Agreement of the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC).
Our Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) strategy is meticulously designed to address pressing climate, environmental, and social challenges, while fostering the Asia Pacific region’s shift to a cleaner energy mix.
Recognizing the complexity of transitioning to a low-carbon economy, particularly in a region heavily dependent on coal for energy, we are dedicated to supporting this transformation over the coming decades.
Success in this endeavor will hinge on effective policies, regulations, and investments that encourage a shift away from fossil fuels towards renewable and innovative energy sources like hydrogen.
In the short to medium term, we acknowledge that thermal coal will continue to play a crucial role in the region’s energy landscape. DMG International is committed to meeting the demand for coal while ensuring that our operations and supply chain practices minimize our climate impact.
In the long term, as the region’s reliance on coal diminishes, we will proactively adapt our business model to remain sustainable and supportive of the energy transition, exploring opportunities to diversify our commodity portfolio.
Our Specialization
Sourcing and Marketing

We possess global coverage on the supply side, underpinned by our strong relationships in key producing areas.

This enables us to effectively source and trade commodities across diverse markets.

Shipping

Our shipping division is bolstered by an independent and highly experienced chartering and operations team.

We manage both in-house and third-party shipping businesses, ensuring efficient and reliable transportation of commodities.

Traffic/Operation

Our operations are comprehensive, covering traffic, scheduling, and quality control.

We have dedicated teams based at both the load port and discharge port to ensure seamless logistics and supply chain management.

Trade Finance

Trade Finance. Our trade finance team is responsible for managing letters of credit (LC) for both import and export transactions.

They also oversee liquidity and cash management, ensuring financial stability and operational efficiency.

Risk Management

We have a robust risk management framework in place, overseeing the monitoring and reporting of both physical and paper positions.

This includes managing related market and credit risks to safeguard our operations and financial health.

Compliance/Legal

Our compliance and legal team is dedicated to handling all legal and regulatory matters related to trading, projects, and investments.

They ensure that all our activities are conducted in accordance with relevant laws and regulations, maintaining our reputation for integrity and ethical conduct.

Together, these specialized areas form the backbone of DMG International's operations, enabling us to excel in the competitive commodity trading market.
